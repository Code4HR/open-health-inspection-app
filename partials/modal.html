Open Health Inspection

Learn More About Where You Eat

This app makes it easy to get public health reports for Virginia restaurants near you. To check the report for a specific restaurant, click its pin on the map or its name in the list.

How to Use this App
Search for a restaurant by name
Browse a list of restaurants near you
Perform a new search from the map center

This app was developed by volunteers working through Code for Hampton Roads.
